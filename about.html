About Us

We are a group of dog fanatics that are dedicated to the love and care of your dog. As dog owners, we understand that we treat our 4-legged frieds as our children. The dream for this company started when two young girls decided that they would be "dog sitters" when they grew up. We know you will not regret entrusting us with your precious pet and friend.
